About

I am Christopher Louie, a designer and programmer based in the Greater Vancouver area. I am current in my 3rd year in the Interactive Arts and Technology program at Simon Fraser University. My professional interests are in game design and development, as well as front and back end development for Android and web.

My work is focused on visual simplicity while also creating simple to read code so that it is easy to modify and build upon.
